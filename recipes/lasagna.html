Lasagna

Description

This is a very basic, very easy to make meat lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds Ground Beef
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 package Mozzerella Cheese
  • 1 container Riccotta Cheese

Steps

  1. Slap meat in tray
  2. Slap cheese on meat
  3. Bake
  4. Profit
